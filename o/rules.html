Rules

Inky SMP is a community-driven server that values respect, integrity, and collaboration. To ensure a positive experience for all players, we have established the following rules:

  1. Be respectful to all players and staff members.
  2. Do not grief or steal from other players.
  3. Do not use hacks, cheats, or exploits.
  4. Do not spam or use inappropriate language.
  5. Do not engage in toxic behavior or harassment.
  6. Follow the instructions of staff members.
  7. Use common courtesy and do not exploit loopholes.
  8. Do not advertise other servers or websites.
  9. Build responsibly and avoid disrupting others' projects.
  10. Keep the chat and builds family-friendly.
  11. Do not abuse glitches or unintended game mechanics.
  12. Respect the environment, avoid excessive land destruction.
