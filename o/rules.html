Rules

Hey there, welcome to Inky SMP! We're all about having a good time and making sure everyone enjoys their stay. To keep things fun and friendly, we've got a few rules we'd like you to follow:

  1. Be cool and respectful to everyone, including staff.
  2. No griefing or stealing, let's keep it fair.
  3. Hacks, cheats, and exploits? Absolutely no exceptions.
  4. Keep the chat clean, no spamming or bad language.
  5. Don't be toxic or harass others. We're here to have fun!
  6. Listen to the staff, they're here to help.
  7. Use common sense and don't try to bend the rules.
  8. No advertising other servers or websites.
  9. Build responsibly and don't mess up others' projects.
  10. Keep everything at least PG13, both chat and builds.
  11. No glitch abuse or unintended game mechanics.
  12. Respect the environment, no excessive land destruction.
  13. No lag machines, please. Let's keep the server running smoothly.
  14. PvP should be consensual, don't attack without agreement.
  15. Report any bugs or issues to the staff ASAP.
  16. Most importantly, have fun and enjoy your time here!

Breaking these rules might get you a warning, a temporary ban, or even a permanent ban. We're all about creating a welcoming and inclusive community, so thanks for helping us keep it that way!

Additional Guidelines

To ensure everyone has a great experience, here are a few more tips to keep in mind:

  • Participate in community events and activities to make new friends.
  • Share your knowledge and help new players get started.
  • Respect other players' builds and give them space to create.
  • Use appropriate skins and usernames that are family-friendly.
  • Keep your builds within the designated areas to avoid conflicts.
  • Listen to staff if they tell you something you're doing is wrong.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or need assistance, feel free to reach out to our staff. We're here to help and ensure you have the best experience possible.

  • Discord: Join our server and reach out to the moderators.
  • iSMP custom annonymous reporting system: Start A Report
  • In-game: Use the /help command to get assistance from online staff.

Thank you for being a part of the Inky SMP community. Let's make this a fun and enjoyable place for everyone!
